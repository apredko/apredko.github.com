Music Is Beautiful

by alanna - Nov 16th, 2012 -

I am the WORST at going to concerts

So I do love music. I really really do, it’s just that I’m not that great at leaving my house to go outside and actually going to the concerts. I don’t know what it is, the standing in a crowd, smelling all these random bodies and feeling their breath all around you. I just can’t motivate myself to go out and actually do it. Oh well. Well now I’ll just buy the EP of the bands I was going to check out and enjoy their music while supported them from the comfort of my own basement.
