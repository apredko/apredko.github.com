Sometimes Things Suck

Nov 26th, 2012

I had a real person day

Today was the day I broke up with a partner of three and a half years. It’s funny thinking of all the time you can spend with a person over long periods of time. Thinking of all the hours and days and weeks all seeping into one long moment in time. I guess for that moment that I sat there I was strong held by my conviction, as I’ve never been one to share words about my feelings verbally. But as I sat there I knew what I was doing, finally able to release the tension in my chest and give into my feelings. All things considered, today was a good day.

I’m in Love With Blue Pencils

Nov 26th, 2012

They’re amazing

So last friday I got something that has seriously changed my life for the better. I got a blue pencil. For months now I’ve been drawing only in pen, convinced that if I couldn’t commit myself to a single line than it didn’t deserve to be drawn. Blue pencils have changed this. I now find great enjoyment in drawing the most delicate line I can, slow sculpting a figure out of nothing instead of getting frustrated at my lack of line perfecting and giving up. I’ve found it most freeing. It gives me new motivation to work on pieces, and that makes me very excited.
